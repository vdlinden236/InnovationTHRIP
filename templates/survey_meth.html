Survey Methodology

A Mixed method approach will be followed which involves both qualitative and quantitative methods. A combination of an online or manual survey, face-to-face leadership interviews and focus groups discussions will be used for the situational analysis. As part of the data collection process, a questionnaire (Annexure B) will be distributed through an online process. Where necessary, the team can facilitate group sessions to support staff members with the completion of the questionnaire.

It is important to note that research indicates that organisations should strive to at least have an overall innovation capability index of 70%, at which stage, the presence of organisational innovation capability builds organisational value. This implies an average score of 70% per innovation pillar. Organisations with an index of 40% or less run the risk of destroying organisational value through a lack of innovation enablement.
